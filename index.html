About CaseCove

Your Trusted Phone Case Partner

CaseCove was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide premium phone protection without compromising on style or performance. What started as a small online store has grown into Europe's leading phone case retailer.

Our PrestaShop e-commerce platform is custom-built for speed and reliability, ensuring your shopping experience is seamless from start to finish. Every case in our collection undergoes rigorous testing for durability, fit, and protection.

We partner directly with manufacturers to bring you the latest innovations in phone protection technology, from military-grade drop protection to eco-friendly materials.
